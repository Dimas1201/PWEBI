Quality Assurance

To ensure the best possible output, we have set up a modern and sophitiscated testing laboratory center in our facility, closely monitoring each step in every manufacturing process. The laboratory is equipped
with appropriate facilities :advanced testing equipment enables accurate analysis of physical and chemical properties of both raw material finished products, and ensures precise adherence to strict guidelines as specified by customers.

Sustainability

Supernova is committed towards a long term sustainable growth. To do so, we understand the importance of taking care of our environment. With the recent interests in eco-friendly products, Supernova has incorporated the latest eco-friendly materials into our production, such as the use of solvent-less ink and food grade materials. To reduce waste materials, Supernova has employed several waste reduction measures to improve material efficiency.
